The Best Times to Go

The best time to go to Destin, FL depends on why you are going...

For clear water, go during Destin's water is clearest in spring (March to May) and fall (September to early November) due to favorable weather and less rain. The clearest water occurs around high tide, especially on days with little to no rainfall. When Destin's water is crystal clear, you might mistake it for the Caribbean!

Crab Island Destin, FL - early June 2024

When NOT to Go

Water runoff from rain can make the water lose its crystal clear appearance. To avoid disappointment, avoid going during or after a rainy time or season. It rains all the time in Florida, but as long as it isn't too heavy it should be okay.
