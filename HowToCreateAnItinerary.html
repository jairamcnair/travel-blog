How to Create an Itinerary

Last Updated: 12-02-2025
Author: Jay B.

This blog post walks through how to create a detailed itinerary for any trip and helps you remember things you might forget.

Introduction

My first piece of general travel advice is to create a detailed itinerary. Even if you are a spontaneous person, it's easier to be spontaneous when you have a default plan.
The further and more expensive the destination, the more I want to do exactly what I want to do while there. The best way to make sure you can do something is to book it before you go; but to book something before you go, you have to know all the details surrounding the schedule. Booking one activity is easy, but what if you've booked two or three activities for a single day? Now you HAVE to iron out every little detail, like when is lunch, how long will it take to travel between activities, and more.
As you can already see, it's easier said than done to plan a trip. Every single detail needs to be accounted for.
